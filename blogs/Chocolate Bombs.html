What is a hot chocolate bomb?
Hot chocolate bombs or hot cocoa bombs are cute lil balls of chocolate that you put into a mug. When you pour hot milk into the mug, the chocolate melts and magically releases the marshmallows and cocoa hiding inside.

  • drinks
  • sweets

It’s super cute and fun and you can make SO many flavor variations! They sell hot chocolate bombs/hot chocolate balls at the store, but around here I haven’t seen any so I decided to make my own. You can too, but you need to get the right molds before it’s too late. Hot chocolate bombs are not only delicious but also a delightful treat to make and enjoy with family and friends. They can be customized with different types of chocolate, fillings, and toppings to suit your taste. Whether you prefer dark, milk, or white chocolate, the possibilities are endless. These bombs make for a perfect gift during the holiday season or any special occasion. Plus, they are a fun and interactive way to enjoy a warm cup of hot chocolate.
